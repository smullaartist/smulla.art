Axiom

  1. Home
  2. Portfolio
  3. Axiom

Axiom was a student film I worked on, where I contributed by designing the sets, capturing behind-the-scenes footage, assisting with equipment and props, and even appearing as an extra in some scenes. I've chosen the best photos from the ones I took on set and showcased them here. The film hasn't been released yet, but once it is, I'll be sure to update this and post it here.

Behind The Process

The film was primarily shot on two sets: one inside a bunker and the other in a tent. The sets were built using flats and backdrops, secured in place with stage braces, hooks, and weights. We decorated the walls with props supplied by our campus's prop department and hung posters which were designed and printed ourselves.

The main actors for the film were current film students whom we reached out to for the project. We cast the extras by recruiting peers and fellow students who were willing to participate. Each cast member had their own outfit, and makeup done to reflect the film’s post-war aesthetic.

The production spanned about a week of nonstop filming, it was an incredible experience to witness and be apart of the inner workings of a film set. I’m excited to take what I’ve learned and apply it to future projects. The film hasn't been released yet, but once it is, I'll be sure to update and post it here.
